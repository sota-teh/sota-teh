Services

What we do

IT services, mostly development. Four flavors — all of them end with software running in production, not with a slide deck.

Custom development

Products and internal tools: desktop, backend, web, mobile, VR — down to hardware integrations. From the first spec to the shipped release, including the parts everyone skips: tests, docs, deployment.

Integration & data

APIs, protocols, data migrations. Making stubborn systems talk to each other — ERP to storefront, device to database, legacy to cloud — and moving the data without losing a single row.

Modernization

Legacy gets respect here — it has carried the business for years. We read it, understand why it is the way it is, and when change is due, propose a refactor that leaves it better, never worse.

Close to the metal

Control and device software — the kind that drives real hardware and must not guess. Serial and network protocols, sensors, actuators, timing budgets. Written with care, reviewed twice, logged always.

How we work

“State of the art” doesn’t mean the newest framework.
It means the one that is still right in ten years.

Senior, small, direct

No account managers, no telephone game, no junior hours billed at senior rates. You talk directly to the engineer whose name is on the commits — so decisions take minutes, not meetings.

Questions before code

A few precise questions beat a month of onboarding: tasks get done right and fast, and the domain knowledge we build up flows back into your process as improvements.

Built to be maintained

Software starts turning into a liability the day after launch — unless it's written, documented and supported to stay an asset. Ours is — by design, not by luck.

Off the clock

We eat our own cooking.

Between client projects we ship our own tools. Pineapple is a tiny Windows tray utility that keeps your machine awake, your presence natural and Windows Power Mode one click away — free, under 1 MB, no installer drama.
